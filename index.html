About Us

Data Science for Anand Good is a non-profit organization dedicated to leveraging data science and analytics to solve pressing social, civic, health, education, and sustainability problems in Anand, Gujarat. We believe that data-driven insights can empower communities, improve public services, and create lasting positive change. Our mission is to bridge the gap between data science expertise and local community needs, ensuring that advanced analytical tools serve the public good.

Projects

Healthcare Analytics
